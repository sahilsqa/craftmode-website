## Phase 3: Outreach & Sales (Days 4-30)

### Targeting Criteria
**Ideal Customer Profile (ICP):**
- 5-50 employees
- $500K-$10M revenue
- No full-time dev team
- Pain: Outdated website or no digital presence
- Industries: Professional services, healthcare, retail, consulting

### Outreach Channels

#### 1. LinkedIn DMs (Primary)
**Daily Target**: 20 DMs/day = 520/month
**Expected**: 5% reply rate = 26 conversations → 5-10 calls → 2-4 clients

**Template (Personalized):**
```
Hi [Name],

Saw [Company] — [specific compliment about their business].

Quick question: Are you happy with how your current website represents [Company]?

I run Craft Mode, an AI-powered agency that builds professional websites in days (not months) at a fraction of agency prices.

Would you be open to seeing a free mockup of how your site could look?

Best,
[Your name]
```

#### 2. Cold Email (Secondary)
**Tool**: Instantly.ai or Smartlead
**Target**: 100 emails/day
**Source**: Apollo.io, Hunter.io

**Sequence**:
- Day 1: Initial outreach
- Day 3: Follow-up
- Day 7: Breakup email

#### 3. Communities (Inbound)
- **IndieHackers**: Share launch, offer help
- **Reddit**: r/smallbusiness, r/entrepreneur (value-first)
- **Slack communities**: Design/dev channels
- **Local business groups**: Chamber of commerce

#### 4. Partnerships
- **Web designers**: White-label dev partner
- **Marketing agencies**: Referral partner
- **Business consultants**: Channel partner
- **Accountants**: Serve their SMB clients

## Phase 4: Launch Tactics (Week 2)

### Product Hunt Launch
- Prepare gallery images
- Write compelling tagline
- Rally friends/upvoters
- Offer PH discount: 20% off

### Beta Client Strategy
**Goal**: 3 clients at discounted rates for testimonials

**Offer**:
- 50% off first 3 clients
- In exchange for: testimonial, case study, referral
- Position as "founding member" pricing

**Where to find them**:
- Your personal network
- Previous employers
- Local businesses you frequent
- Twitter/LinkedIn announcements
