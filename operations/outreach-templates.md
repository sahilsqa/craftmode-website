# Craft Mode — Outreach Templates

## 💼 LinkedIn DM Templates

### Template 1: Website Refresh Angle
```
Hi {FirstName},

Came across {Company} — love what you're doing in {industry}.

Quick question: when you look at your current website, does it match how far your business has actually come?

I run Craft Mode, an AI-powered agency that rebuilds websites in 7-10 days for $1,500-3,500.

Same quality as a $20K agency project. Fraction of the cost and time.

Worth a 10-minute chat to see if it's a fit?

Best,
{YourName}
```

### Template 2: Direct Pain Point
```
Hi {FirstName},

Noticed {Company} has been growing fast — congrats!

Quick question: is your digital presence keeping up with your growth?

I help SMBs like yours build professional websites and apps using AI — delivered in days, not months, at a fraction of agency prices.

Starting at $1,500 with a free mockup in 48 hours.

Interested in seeing what we could build for {Company}?

{YourName}
```

### Template 3: Referral Request
```
Hi {FirstName},

Hope you're doing well!

Quick ask: I just launched Craft Mode — an AI agency that builds websites/apps for SMBs at 1/10th the cost of traditional agencies.

Do you know any business owners who:
• Need a new website
• Got sticker shock from agency quotes
• Are launching something new

I'd love an intro. Happy to return the favor however I can!

{YourName}
```

## 📧 Cold Email Sequences

```
Subject: {Company} website + quick question

I was looking at {Company}'s website and compared it to {Competitor}.

A few opportunities stood out to improve conversion rate.

I'm {YourName} from Craft Mode — we use AI to rebuild websites in 7-10 days.

Would you be open to seeing a free mockup of how {Company} could look?

Best,
{YourName}
Craft Mode | craftmode.co

```

{YourName}
