FAQ

Questions? Answered.

How is this different from hiring a traditional agency?
Traditional agencies bill by the hour and take 2-3 months to deliver. We use AI to work 10x faster, which means you get the same (or better) quality in 1-2 weeks at a fraction of the cost. No bloated teams, no overhead—just results.
What does "unlimited revisions" actually mean?
It means we keep refining until you're 100% happy. Because we use AI, iterations are fast and cost us almost nothing—so we pass that benefit to you. Most clients are satisfied within 2-3 rounds, but we're not counting.
Will my website be SEO-optimized?
Absolutely. Every site we build includes technical SEO best practices: fast loading speeds, mobile optimization, proper heading structure, meta tags, schema markup, and XML sitemaps. We also offer advanced SEO packages for businesses that want to dominate search rankings.
What happens after my project is launched?
We don't disappear after launch. Every project includes 30 days of support for bug fixes and minor tweaks. After that, we offer affordable maintenance packages or can train your team to manage the site. For marketing retainers, we're with you every month analyzing and optimizing.
Do you work with businesses outside the US?
Yes! We work with SMBs across the US and English-speaking Europe (UK, Ireland, Netherlands, Nordics). Our team is comfortable with time zone differences and we understand regional nuances like GDPR compliance for European businesses.
